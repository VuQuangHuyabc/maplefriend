Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What are Maple Friend scented candles made from?

Our products are made from pure soy wax, natural essential oils, and premium cotton wicks, safe for your health.

❓ How long does delivery take?

HCMC: 2-4 hours. Other provinces: 1-3 business days. We also offer express delivery if needed.

❓ Is there a return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days if products have manufacturing defects or don't match the description.

❓ How to preserve scented candles?

Store in a dry place, avoid direct sunlight and high temperatures. Cover when not in use to preserve fragrance longer.
