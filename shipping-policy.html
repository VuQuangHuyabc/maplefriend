Shipping Policy FAQs

What is the preparation time?

Our preparation time for orders is 1 day.

What is the shipping time?

Shipping typically takes 2–3 days after preparation.

Do you offer expedited shipping?

Currently, we do not offer expedited shipping options. Standard shipping is 2–3 days.

How can I track my order?

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.

What shipping carriers do you use?

We use reliable carriers to ensure timely delivery of your orders.

Can I change my shipping address?

Please contact us immediately if you need to change your shipping address before your order is shipped.
