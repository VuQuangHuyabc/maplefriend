About Maple Friend

The story of passion for scented candles and the art of living

Our Story

Maple Friend was founded in 2020 with the mission to bring premium scented candle products that not only beautify living spaces but also nurture the soul.

The name "Maple Friend" symbolizes warm, close friendship like autumn maple leaves - we want every Maple Friend product to become a companion, bringing peace and relaxation to our customers.

After more than 3 years of establishment and development, Maple Friend has become a beloved scented candle brand in Vietnam, with thousands of trusted customers and high appreciation.

Vision & Mission

🎯 Vision

To become the leading scented candle brand in Vietnam, bringing quality living spaces and elevating the fragrance experience for every Vietnamese family.

🌟 Mission

To create high-quality scented candle products from natural ingredients, safe for health and environmentally friendly.

💎 Core Values

Quality: Always prioritize product quality above all.
Creativity: Continuously research and develop new fragrances.
Customer: Put customers at the center of all activities.
